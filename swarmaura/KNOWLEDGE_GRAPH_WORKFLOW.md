## **­Ъј» Knowledge Graph Overview**

The HivePath AI Knowledge Graph is the **digital brain** that powers our entire logistics intelligence system. It's not just a static databaseРђћit's a living, breathing network that continuously learns, adapts, and evolves to understand the complex relationships in your logistics operations.
